OPSEC & Privacy Guides

Basic OPSEC Principles

  • Use separate identities and emails for sensitive activities.
  • Use strong, unique passwords + password manager.
  • Always enable 2FA where possible (preferably with hardware keys).
  • Use VPNs that don’t keep logs and support anonymous payment.
  • Prefer privacy-focused email providers, avoid big US-based ones.
  • Use burner phones or eSIMs purchased anonymously.
  • Keep devices updated and minimize metadata leakage.
  • Use end-to-end encrypted messaging apps without phone numbers (Session, SimpleX, Briar).
  • Avoid linking real identity with pseudonymous accounts.

Staying Anonymous While Gaming

Gaming platforms like Steam typically require email verification and sometimes phone verification. To improve privacy:

  • Create a separate Steam account with privacy-focused email.
  • Use VPN when logging in.
  • Avoid linking payment methods to real identity (use crypto if possible).
  • Limit friend list and public profile visibility.

Handling Government Subpoenas & EDR

Using offshore services with no logs and anonymous payment can greatly reduce subpoena risk. However:

  • Always encrypt your data locally before uploading.
  • Use multi-hop VPN or Tor for extra anonymity.
  • Use pseudonyms, avoid sharing personal info.
  • Use ephemeral emails and accounts for risky operations.

Remember, no method is 100% safe if extreme pressure is applied.
