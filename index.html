Quick OPSEC Tips for 2025

  • Use reputable no-log VPNs like Mullvad, IVPN, or AzireVPN to hide your IP.
  • Use privacy-respecting email providers such as Tutanota or ProtonMail, and avoid Gmail or Outlook.
  • Prefer cash or cryptocurrency payments to minimize payment traces.
  • Use burner devices or VMs for risky activities.
  • Enable 2FA and strong passwords on all accounts.
  • Keep your software updated and avoid sketchy downloads.
  • Use secure messaging apps like Signal or Session for private communication.
  • Be careful what metadata you share in files and photos.
  • Use Tor or bridges for extra anonymity in sensitive cases.
  • Regularly audit your OPSEC habits and improve where needed.
